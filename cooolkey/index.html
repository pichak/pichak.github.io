Cooolkey

CooolKey is a customizable keyboard. It enables users to change the theme, color, button shape, font, sound and even animation of typing. These features made CooolKey a perfect and popular keyboard. One distinctive feature of CooolKey is its social platfrom which allows users to share keyboard themes with their friends!

Cooolkey ranked #1 in the Apple’s app store the day after its launch, on top of apps like Facebook, Twitter and Gmail. We reached over 2 million downloads just in two days. It was a big success which made us think bigger.
