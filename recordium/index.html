Recordium

Recordium is a powerful yet elegant recording app designed for iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch. Recordium's unique highlight function allows you to get to the heart of your audio and find the parts that really matter. It enriches your recordings with as many handy tags, searchable notes, and helpful pictures as you want with just a tap of your fingers.

This app did break into the top 10 apps in 53 countries in the Apple's app store following the day we launched it.
